About The Hackathon

RiverHacks is a yearly Austin Community College hackathon, first established in 2022. Open to all ACC students and alumni, this 24-hour event challenges you to team up and build a product relevant to the Austin area. During this hybrid hackathon, you will acquire valuable skills, gain valuable experience, and enjoy some delicious food on us! Whether you're a novice or an experienced hacker, we'll provide you with the guidance and all the necessary tools to assist you along the way!

F.A.Q

  • Who can attend?

    Any student currently enrolled in Austin Community College. As long as you have a passion for learning, creating something new, and having a great time, you can attend.

  • All the doors are shut, how can I enter the building?

    You may enter the 2000 bldg facing ACC Highland Campus Dr. street and enter from the second floor where the information desk is located. You may also enter from the 1000 bldg facing the parking lot and enter from the first floor where the information desk is located.

  • How much coding experience do I need?

    None! We are looking for people who are passionate about coding and want to learn more, and we will provide mentors, and support to help you build your project. You'll also have the opportunity to work with teammates who have more experience and are willing to share their knowledge.

  • What should I bring?

    Please bring a valid ID as it will be required to check-in at the ACC Highland campus. In addition please bring a laptop, mouse, charger, jacket, and any other supplies you may need for your project. We will provide you with meals, snacks, and drinks.

  • How much do I have to pay?

    Nothing! In fact, we'll be giving out free food, snacks, and drinks to all attendees. We'll even have prizes for our winners!

  • When is the deadline to apply?

    May 17th, 11:59PM CST

  • How do teams work?

    Teams are made up of up to 4 people. You can apply as a team, but if you don't have a team, you will be matched with a team of your own.

  • Will River Hacks be in person?

    RiverHacks is a hybrid hackathon. All ceremonies and events will be held in-person, but will also be streamed live to virtual attendees. Virtual attendees are also eligible for prizes.

  • Will this be an overnight event?

    Due to ACC policy, we will be required to leave Highland Campus at 9:00PM and return the following morning at 9:00AM.

  • When can I start my project?

    All work must be done during the hacking period detailed in the schedule. You can't demo anything you didn't build. To be transparent about your work, utilizing version control is recommended.

  • Will there be prizes?

    Yes! We will have prizes for the top 3 placing teams. The winners can choose which prize they want of the three selections until they are all taken.
